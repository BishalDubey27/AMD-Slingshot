# NutriSmart - Pitch Deck

## Slide 1: The Problem 🎯

### 70% of college students struggle with nutrition
- Skip meals due to busy schedules
- Lack awareness of what they're eating
- No easy way to track and improve habits
- Poor nutrition affects academic performance and mental health

### Current Solutions Fall Short
- Complex calorie counting apps (too tedious)
- Expensive nutritionists (not accessible)
- Generic diet plans (don't adapt to individual needs)

---

## Slide 2: Our Solution 💡

### NutriSmart: Your AI-Powered Food Companion

**"Just tell us what you ate, we'll handle the rest"**

- Simple natural language input (no databases to search)
- Instant nutrition insights
- Smart, personalized recommendations
- Privacy-first (all data stays on your device)

---

## Slide 3: How It Works ⚙️

### 3 Simple Steps

1. **Set Your Goals**
   - Weight loss, muscle gain, or maintain health
   - Define your calorie target
   - Specify dietary preferences

2. **Log Your Meals**
   - Type what you ate in plain language
   - "Chicken biryani" or "Paneer sandwich"
   - AI estimates nutrition automatically

3. **Get Smart Insights**
   - Real-time calorie tracking
   - Personalized recommendations
   - Behavioral nudges at the right time

### Intelligent Recommendations
- Context-aware suggestions based on eating patterns
- Time-based nudges (breakfast reminders, meal timing)
- Goal-specific guidance

### Indian Food Intelligence
- Recognizes dosa, biryani, dal, paneer, and more
- Culturally relevant suggestions

### Privacy First

## Slide 5: The Technology 🔧

### Built for Scale & Impact

**Frontend**: Pure JavaScript (ES6+), HTML5, CSS3

**Storage**: Browser LocalStorage (privacy-first)

### Future Enhancements
